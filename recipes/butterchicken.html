Tasty Butter Chicken

A rich, creamy, and mildly spiced North Indian dish where tender chicken pieces are simmered in a luscious tomato-butter sauce. Perfect with naan or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup tomato puree (or crushed tomatoes)
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream (or coconut cream)
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of tikka masala spice mix (store bought)

Instructions

  1. Cook chicken:

    2. In a pan, melt 1tbsp of butter. Add chicken and cook until browned.

  2. Add sauce:

    3. Stir in tomato puree and spice mix. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.

  3. Finish:

    4. Add remaining butter and cream. Stir and simmer until thick and creamy.

  4. Serve:

    5. Garnish with little Extra cream and enjoy with naan bread or rice.
